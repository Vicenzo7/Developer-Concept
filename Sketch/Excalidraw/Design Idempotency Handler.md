Design Idempotency Handler ^hvoJNKCX

Idempotency helps to take care of duplicate request.

It enables client to safely retry operation without worrying the side effect 
of the operation that can cause.

By default GET,PUT and DELETE are idempotent in nature
POST request is not idempotent and we have to make it idempotent.

GET,PUT and DELETE will not change the DB value with multiple same request,
but POST will. ^TGkukuxj
